Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own drone to use AgriVision?

No, AgriVision partners with local drone service providers in Zambia to offer scanning services. You can also use your own compatible drone with our Pro and Enterprise plans.

How does AgriVision work without internet?

Our app’s core features, including disease detection and IoT data storage, work offline. Data syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

What crops are supported in Zambia?

AgriVision supports major Zambian crops like maize, soybeans, tomatoes, groundnuts, and cassava. Pro and Enterprise plans cover 50+ crops, including local varieties.

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our AI achieves 95% accuracy for common diseases and pests in Zambian crops, with ongoing improvements through local data training.

Can AgriVision integrate with my existing tools?

Yes, our Enterprise plan offers API access for integration with farm management systems, and Pro users can export data for analysis.
