Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free to download?

Yes, the app is free to download. You can start with our 30-day free trial to experience all features.

Can I use the app without a drone?

Yes, you can use the app's basic features without a drone. We partner with local drone service providers for scanning services.

How much data does the app use?

The app is optimized for low data usage. Most features work offline, and data sync happens only when connected to WiFi.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your farm data. Your information is never shared without your permission.
